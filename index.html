Who we are

Jennings Tactical Guns is a Central Texas based firearms provider.
Family owned and operated by active Law Enforcement, Military and Competition Shooters.
We aim to provide solid accurate and dependable hunting, competition and self defense firearms.

What we offer

We can provide parts and tools for your next build or we can talk about your needs and build your next firearm for you.

We are distributors for Aero Precision, Ballistic Advantage, Spikes Tactical, Stag Arms and CMC Triggers.
We can also get most models of any brand of new guns.


With select barrels and parts we can guarantee sub MOA accuracy with match grade ammunition.
